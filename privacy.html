Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

At Kingdom Reality Ministries, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and respect. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our ministry.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Contact Us: When you fill out contact forms, prayer request forms, or reach out via email, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any information you share in your message.
  • Register for Events: When you register for events, conferences, or programs, we collect your name, contact information, and any other details necessary for your participation.
  • Join a Ministry: When you express interest in joining a ministry, we collect information to help us connect you with the right ministry leaders.
  • Make Donations: When you give financially, we collect payment information necessary to process your donation securely. We do not store credit card details on our servers.
  • Subscribe to Communications: When you subscribe to our newsletter or updates, we collect your email address and preferences.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website addresses
  • Device information (operating system, device type)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Respond to Inquiries: Answer your questions, pray for your requests, and provide the information or assistance you need.
  • To Communicate: Send you updates about events, services, and ministry activities that may interest you.
  • To Process Registrations: Manage your participation in events, conferences, and ministry programs.
  • To Improve Our Website: Understand how visitors interact with our site to enhance user experience.
  • To Fulfill Legal Obligations: Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within Our Ministry: With ministry leaders and volunteers who need the information to serve you effectively.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and ministry (e.g., email delivery services, payment processors). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption on our website
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information within our ministry
  • Secure data storage practices

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from our communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@kingdomreality.org.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our site
  • Improve site functionality

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

7. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. Our website and ministries include content for children (such as Sunday School), but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., social media platforms, Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Mail: Kingdom Reality Ministries, Nairobi, Kenya
