Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What time should I arrive for service?

We recommend arriving 15-20 minutes before service starts, especially if it's your first visit. This gives you time to park, find a seat, and get settled before worship begins.

Is there parking available?

Yes! We have free on-site parking with security personnel. There's also additional parking across the street if needed.

What should I wear?

Come as you are! Whether you prefer formal attire or casual clothes, you're welcome. We care more about your heart than your outfit.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes! We have children's church during Sunday services, as well as youth programs throughout the week for different age groups.

How can I join a ministry?

Fill out the contact form above or speak to any ministry leader after service. We'd love to help you find your place of service.

Is the church accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, our facility is wheelchair accessible with ramps and designated seating areas. Please let an usher know how we can assist you.
