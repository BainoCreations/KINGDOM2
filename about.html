Our Story

Kingdom Reality Ministries was birthed from a divine encounter and a burning passion to see God's people walk in the fullness of their identity in Christ. Founded in 2010, what began as a small home fellowship has grown into a vibrant community of believers committed to living out Kingdom principles.

The name "Kingdom Reality" was given through a prophetic word - a call to shift believers from religious routines to authentic Kingdom living. We believe that every believer is destined to demonstrate the reality of God's Kingdom in their sphere of influence.

Over the years, we've seen countless lives transformed, families restored, and destinies fulfilled as people encounter the truth of who they are in Christ. Our journey is a testament to God's faithfulness and His desire to establish His Kingdom on earth as it is in heaven.

Our Ten Pillars

The foundational principles that Kingdom Reality Ministries is built upon

Pray

We are committed to a lifestyle of prayer, knowing that everything flows from constant communication with our Father.

Learn

We dedicate ourselves to studying God's Word, growing in wisdom, and understanding Kingdom principles.

Honor

We honor God, our leaders, one another, and those who have gone before us, creating a culture of respect.

Project

We dream big and execute Kingdom projects that impact our community and expand God's influence.

Connect

We build authentic relationships, connecting people to God, to the church, and to one another.

Outreach

We take the love of Christ beyond our walls, reaching the lost and serving the needy.

Give

We practice generous and cheerful giving, honoring God with our finances and resources.

Support

We uphold one another in times of need, providing emotional, spiritual, and practical support.

Home

We create a spiritual family where everyone belongs, feels safe, and can grow in a loving environment.

Serve

We follow Jesus' example of servanthood, humbly using our gifts to serve the church and community.

Unless the Lord builds the house, the builders labor in vain. These are the pillars upon which we build our church.
