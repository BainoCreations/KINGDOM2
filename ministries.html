Mercy Ministry

Compassion in Action

"Blessed are the merciful, for they will be shown mercy." — Matthew 5:7

The Mercy Ministry is the compassionate heart of our church, reaching out to the most vulnerable in our community. We believe that God's love is demonstrated through tangible acts of kindness, care, and support to those who are suffering, marginalized, or in crisis.

What We Do:

  • Weekly food distribution to needy families
  • Hospital visitation and prayer for the sick
  • Support for widows, orphans, and single mothers
  • Emergency relief during crises and disasters
  • Prison ministry and rehabilitation support
  • Clothing drives for the less fortunate

Meeting Times:

Every Saturday: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Outreach)

1st Tuesday Monthly: 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM (Planning & Prayer)

👑 Kings Ministry

Raising Royal Priesthood

"But you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, God's special possession." — 1 Peter 2:9

The Kings Ministry is dedicated to building men of integrity, strength, and purpose. We equip men to lead their families, communities, and workplaces with godly wisdom, courage, and servant leadership. Our focus is on raising men who understand their identity as sons of the King.

What We Do:

  • Men's Bible study and fellowship
  • Annual men's retreat: "Warrior's Encounter"
  • Mentorship and discipleship programs
  • Business and career networking
  • Fitness and wellness accountability groups
  • Community service and repair projects

Meeting Times:

Every Saturday: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM (Men's Breakfast & Fellowship)

2nd & 4th Wednesday: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM (Bible Study)

👑 Queens Ministry

Daughters of the King

"The King is enthralled by your beauty; honor him, for he is your Lord." — Psalm 45:11

The Queens Ministry celebrates and empowers women to walk in their God-given identity, dignity, and destiny. We create a safe and nurturing space for women to grow spiritually, emotionally, and socially. Through authentic relationships and teaching, we help women discover their worth as daughters of the King.

What We Do:

  • Annual women's conferences and retreats
  • Monthly tea and fellowship gatherings
  • Prayer circles and mentorship programs
  • Creative arts and crafts ministry
  • Outreach to single mothers and widows
  • Book clubs and Bible studies

Meeting Times:

Every 1st Saturday: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Tea & Testimony)

Every Wednesday: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Bible Study)

⚡ SHINE YOUTH ⚡

IGNITE THE FIRE

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity." — 1 Timothy 4:12

SHINE Youth Church is where young people encounter God in a real, relevant, and RADICAL way! We're not just the future of the church—we're the church NOW! With vibrant worship, relevant teaching, and genuine community, we create an atmosphere where young people can encounter God and discover their purpose.

WHAT WE DO:

  • High-energy worship nights with live band
  • Gaming tournaments and fun days
  • Outdoor adventures and camps
  • Open mic and talent showcases
  • Youth-led community projects
  • Pizza and Pod (small group discussions)
  • Movie nights and lock-ins

FRIDAY NIGHTS: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM (Main Youth Service)

SUNDAYS: 11:00 AM (Youth Church during main service)

✨ Sunday School ✨

Building Faith from the Start

"Jesus said, 'Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.'" — Matthew 19:14

Our Sunday School is a fun, safe, and nurturing environment where children learn about God's love through creative activities, Bible stories, and meaningful relationships. We believe that laying a strong spiritual foundation in childhood shapes the adults they become. Every child is valued, loved, and taught that they are special to God.

What We Do:

  • Creative Bible lessons with crafts
  • Fun worship songs and dance
  • Puppet shows and Bible skits
  • Bible games and activities
  • Birthday celebrations
  • Nature walks and exploring God's creation
  • Vacation Bible School (holidays)

Age Groups:

Toddlers (2-3 yrs) Preschool (4-5 yrs) Early Elementary (6-8 yrs) Upper Elementary (9-11 yrs)

Meeting Times:

Every Sunday: 9:00 AM & 11:00 AM (During Main Services)

Monthly Family Day: Last Sunday of Month (Parents join)
